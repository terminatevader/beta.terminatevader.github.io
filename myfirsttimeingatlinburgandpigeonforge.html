My First Time In Gatlinburg And Pigeon Forge

TermiNateVader

Hello everyone today in this blog I am going to talk about my first time in Gatlinburg and Pigeon Forge. Before I got there I thought both cities were almost identical because I have heard them referred to as twin cities, but going there I saw that both cities were totally different from each other. Pigeon Forge seemed to have a lot more buildings than Gatlinburg. The traffic in both cities was horrible, but that's to be expected because it was during fall break and they are both tourist destinations. The public transportation in Gatlinburg was the best public transportation I have been on. We did not get to do a whole lot because we were not there for very long, so we will have to plan another trip to get the full experience.
